The Vitra Campus is a playing field – but for serious games.

The Vitra collection of architecture by the great architects of the present day is unique in the world. With works by such prominent architects as Nicholas Grimshaw, Frank Gehry, Zaha Hadid, Tadao Ando, Álvaro Siza, Herzog & de Neuron and SANAA, the Vitra Campus are regarded as a model of contemporary industrial culture and integrated brand identity in which design plays a central role.
